## 2. Blocker Analysis

For each blocker, identify:

- **Impact** - What it's preventing
- **Owner** - Who can resolve it
- **Dependencies** - What else it affects
- **Solution** - How to unblock (if known)

**TYPE 2: Coordination**

**TYPE 3: Information**
- Missing contact info

- Decision needed

**TYPE 4: Technical**
- System issues
- Tool problems
