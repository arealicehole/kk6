## SECTION 1: EVENT PAGE vs GROUP - WHICH TO USE?

### The Dual Strategy (Recommended)

**Use Both Together:**
- **Event Page:** For broadcasting information, RSVPs, logistics, ad traffic
- **Facebook Group:** For community interaction, daily engagement, donor spotlights

**Why Both?**
- Event Pages get better organic reach and discoverability
- Groups create deeper engagement and community (73% avg engagement rate for pages posting 1-2x daily)
- Event Pages work seamlessly with Facebook Ads
- Groups foster user-generated content and peer motivation

**Linking Strategy:**
- Pin Group invite link at top of Event Page
- Post Event Page updates in Group with "RSVP here" CTAs
- Cross-promote: "Join our Group for daily updates and behind-the-scenes"

### Event Page Strengths
- Public visibility and SEO
- RSVP tracking and conversion metrics
- Co-host feature (add Sojourner Center, vendors, box hosts as co-hosts)
- Native integration with Facebook Ads
- Appears on partners' pages when they accept co-host invites

### Group Strengths
- Many-to-many communication (members talk to each other)
- Higher engagement rates than Pages
- User-generated content hub (donor photos, box location visits)
- Gamification opportunities (leaderboards, challenges)
- Privacy options (closed groups foster belonging)

**DECISION:** Create Event Page (already exists) + NEW Private/Closed Group for KK6 community.

## SECTION 6: CANNABIS COMPLIANCE (CRITICAL)

### Facebook's Cannabis Policy (2024)

**What's Allowed:**
- ‚úÖ Community events that happen to be cannabis-themed
- ‚úÖ Promoting event attendance (no product sales)
- ‚úÖ Educational content about cannabis culture
- ‚úÖ CBD/hemp product promotion (WITH Meta approval + LegitScript certification)
- ‚úÖ Highlighting dispensary involvement in community events

**What's BANNED:**
- ‚ùå Selling, trading, or facilitating delivery of THC products
- ‚ùå Direct promotions, discounts, or sales pitches for cannabis products
- ‚ùå Implying products are for sale at event
- ‚ùå Advertising cannabis products (even if legal in AZ)
- ‚ùå Allowing minors to view cannabis-related content

**Arizona-Specific Restrictions:**
- Cannabis businesses can promote their BRAND but NOT products
- Event promotion is allowed (as long as 21+ only)
- Private social media accounts must state followers should be 21+
- No billboards, TV, radio ads for cannabis products (but events are different)

## SECTION 12: HANDLING NEGATIVE COMMENTS & TROLLS

### Comment Moderation Strategy

**Negative Comment Types + Responses:**

**Type 1: "This is promoting drug use!"**
Response:
```
KannaKickback is a community event celebrating cannabis culture (legal in Arizona for adults 21+) while supporting the Sojourner Center's mission to help Phoenix families. Our focus is on charitable giving and bringing people together. Since 2020, we've donated $20K+ in toys. We'd love for you to join us in making a difference! üéÅ
```

**Type 2: "Why would you mix weed and charity?"**
Response:
```
Great question! The cannabis community is incredibly generous and community-oriented. KannaKickback proves that fun and philanthropy go hand-in-hand. Our attendees have donated thousands of toys over the years, directly benefiting families supported by the Sojourner Center. Different communities give back in different ways‚Äîthis is ours! üíö
```

**Type 3: "This is inappropriate for kids."**
Response:
```
KannaKickback is an adult event (21+ only) with a charitable mission. While the party is cannabis-friendly, the toy donations go to children through the Sojourner Center, a respected Phoenix nonprofit. We take both our community culture and our charitable impact seriously. Thank you for your concern!
```

**Type 4: Spam/Solicitation**
Action:
- Delete immediately
- Ban user if repeat offender
- Do NOT engage

**Type 5: "I reported this event for promoting drugs."**
Response:
```
We understand cannabis can be a sensitive topic. To clarify: KannaKickback does not sell or facilitate the sale of any cannabis products. We're hosting a community toy drive and celebration for adults 21+, which is fully compliant with Facebook's policies and Arizona law. Our focus is on supporting the Sojourner Center and bringing the community together. If you have questions, feel free to DM us directly. ‚úåÔ∏è
```

### Moderation Workflow

**In Event Page:**
- Monitor comments 2x daily (morning + evening)
- Respond to questions within 24 hours
- Hide (don't delete) borderline negative comments
- Delete only: spam, hate speech, or clear policy violations

**In Group:**
- Moderators monitor throughout the day
- Address negativity quickly (within 2-4 hours)
- Private message repeat offenders before banning
- Ban criteria: Hate speech, harassment, repeated spam, product sales attempts

**Escalation Protocol:**
1. First offense: Public response (polite, informative)
2. Second offense: Private message warning
3. Third offense: Remove from Group / hide from Event
4. Persistent issues: Ban + report to Facebook if needed

**Example:**
