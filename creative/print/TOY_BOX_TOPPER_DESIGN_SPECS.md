# Toy Donation Box Topper - Design Specifications

**Created:** 2025-11-17
**Purpose:** Physical sign for top of toy donation boxes at dispensaries and smoke shops
**Format:** 16:9 horizontal landscape
**Dimensions:** 1920x1080px (high resolution for print)

## Visual Style
- Fun and festive holiday theme
- Cannabis-friendly but family-appropriate
- Similar style to existing KannaKickback 6 social media graphics
- KannaKlaus character (Santa in red suit with cannabis leaves, peace signs, gift bag)

## Brand Colors
- Forest green: #2D5016
- Holiday red: #C41E3A
- Gold: #FFD700
- White for contrast and text

## Text Content (Priority Order)

### 1. HEADLINE (Largest, Most Prominent)
**"TOY DONATION BOX"**
- Bold, impossible to miss
- High contrast colors

### 2. SUBHEAD
**"KannaKickback 6 - Benefiting Sojourner Center"**

### 3. CALL TO ACTION
**"Drop a NEW, UNWRAPPED TOY"**
- Clear instruction
- Friendly but direct

### 4. DATES
**"Toy Drive: Nov 15 - Dec 15, 2025"**

### 5. EVENT INFO
**"Celebrate with us Dec 6, 2-6pm at Ginza, Gold Canyon"**

### 6. SOCIAL HANDLE
**"@kannakrew"**

### 7. QR CODE PLACEHOLDER
- White square box labeled "QR CODE"
- Add text: "Scan for details"
- Bottom corner placement
- User will manually add QR code later (links to kannakickback.com)

## Layout Requirements

### Character Placement
- KannaKlaus character on left OR right side
- Character should be prominent but not overwhelming
- Peace sign hands up, gift bag visible
- Red Santa suit with cannabis leaf details

### Headline Placement
- "TOY DONATION BOX" in center or top-center
- Must be the first thing people see
- Bold, thick font
- High contrast (white text on dark background OR dark text with white outline)

### Decorative Elements
- Festive holiday decorations (snowflakes, ornaments, lights)
- Toy/gift box graphics
- Christmas trees (optional)
- Gold stars or sparkles
- Keep festive but not cluttered

### QR Code Area
- Bottom right or bottom left corner
- White square placeholder
- "QR CODE" text inside square
- "Scan for details" text nearby
- Adequate white space around it

### Information Hierarchy
1. TOY DONATION BOX (largest)
2. Call to action + KannaKlaus character (prominent)
3. KannaKickback 6 branding + Sojourner Center mention
4. Dates and event details
5. Social handle and QR code

## Technical Specs
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Resolution: 1920x1080px minimum
- DPI: 300 for print quality
- File format: PNG with transparency support
- Color mode: RGB

- Existing KannaKickback 6 social media graphics show the character style
- KannaKlaus: Red Santa suit, white beard, cannabis leaf decorations, peace sign hands, gift bag
- Holiday color scheme: Red, green, gold with white accents
- Festive background with lights, snowflakes, ornaments
