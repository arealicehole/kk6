## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Format:
- **Size:** 8.5" x 11" (standard letter) OR 11" x 17" (poster size)
  - Recommend 11x17 for maximum visibility
- **Orientation:** Portrait
- **Color:** Full color (CMYK for print)
- **Bleed:** 0.125" bleed on all sides
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Format:** PDF (print-ready) + JPG (digital sharing)

### Paper:
- **Stock:** 100lb gloss cover OR 80lb cardstock
- **Finish:** Gloss or matte (gloss recommended for vibrant colors)

---

## VISUAL DESIGN CONCEPT

### Primary Visual:
**KannaKlaus as the hero**
- Festive Santa/cannabis mashup character
- Holding toys and cannabis leaf
- Big, friendly, approachable vibe
- Should be illustrated/AI-generated (not photo-realistic)

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** Forest green (#2D5016), holiday red (#C41E3A)
- **Accent:** Festive gold (#FFD700), cannabis green (#4CAF50)
- **Background:** Deep red OR cream/off-white (NOT pure white)
- **Text:** High contrast (white on dark, black on light)

### Typography:
- **Headers:** Bold, festive fonts (think holiday poster energy)
- **Body:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Emphasis:** "KANNA KICKBACK 6" should be HUGE and unmissable

### Layout Style:
- Eye-catching, scrollstopping design
- NOT too cluttered (needs to be readable from 6 feet away)
- Festival/party poster vibe (fun, not corporate)
- Cannabis-friendly but NOT stereotypical stoner design
- Should work in color AND black/white (for photocopying)
