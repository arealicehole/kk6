## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Image Specs
- **Format:** PNG
- **Dimensions:** 1080x1080 pixels (Instagram square)
- **Resolution:** 72 DPI minimum (300 DPI preferred for quality)
- **Color Mode:** RGB
- **File Size:** Under 8MB per image

### Typography Guidelines
- **Primary Font:** Sans-serif, bold/extra-bold weights (Impact, Montserrat, Bebas Neue)
- **Body Font:** Clean sans-serif (Open Sans, Roboto, Lato)
- **Readability:** High contrast between text and background
- **Hierarchy:** Clear size/weight differences between headline, subtext, body

### Accessibility
- **Contrast Ratio:** Minimum 4.5:1 for all text
- **Text Size:** Large enough to read on mobile (minimum 36px)
- **Alt Text Ready:** Descriptive text for each slide

---

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **Primary:** Main headline (largest, boldest)
2. **Secondary:** Subtext or supporting info (medium weight)
3. **Tertiary:** Details, website, small text (smallest but readable)

### Color Strategy
- **Background:** Use greens for energy/excitement, white for clarity
- **Text:** High contrast - white on dark, dark on light
- **Accents:** Gold for emphasis, red sparingly for urgency
- **Balance:** Don't overwhelm with too many colors per slide

### Cannabis Culture Balance
- **Subtle Integration:** Leaf motifs, green color palette
- **Not Overwhelming:** This is a charity toy drive first
- **Approachable:** Welcoming to all community members
- **Professional:** Respectable business partnerships
