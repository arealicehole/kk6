## SUCCESS CRITERIA

**This ad will be successful if:**
- CTR (Click-Through Rate) > 1.5%
- CPC (Cost Per Click) < $0.50
- Viewer retention > 50% at 10-second mark
- Completion rate > 30%
- Positive comments mentioning charity/Sojourner Center

**Hypothesis:**
Charity-forward messaging will attract viewers motivated by community impact, resulting in higher-quality RSVPs (people more likely to actually attend and bring toys). May have slightly higher CPC but better conversion quality.
