# KannaKickback 6 - Carousel Ad Creative Brief
## Version B: PARTY-FORWARD

**Campaign:** KannaKickback 6 Paid Facebook/Instagram Ads
**Format:** 5-Card Carousel (Square Images)
**Date Created:** 2025-11-26
**A/B Test Hypothesis:** Party-focused carousel highlighting fun, food, and community will drive higher volume RSVPs from social/entertainment-motivated audiences.

---

## CAROUSEL SPECIFICATIONS

**Technical Requirements:**
- **Number of Cards:** 5
- **Image Size:** 1080 x 1080 pixels (square) for each card
- **File Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File Size:** Under 30MB per image
- **Total Carousel Size:** Under 150MB
- **Placements:** Instagram Feed, Facebook Feed (Carousel not available in Stories)

**Carousel Behavior:**
- Users swipe left to advance through cards (mobile)
- Users click arrows to advance (desktop)
- Each card can have unique headline and description
- All cards link to same destination URL

## DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Typography
**Headline Font (on images):**
- Sans-serif, bold, modern
- Recommended: Montserrat ExtraBold, Raleway Black, Oswald Bold
- Size: 90-140pt (bigger/bolder than Version A)

**Body Text Font:**
- Sans-serif, medium-bold weight
- Recommended: Open Sans SemiBold, Lato Bold, Poppins Medium
- Size: 45-65pt

**Accent Text:**
- Same as body font
- Size: 35-45pt

### Color Specifications
**Text Colors:**
- Primary: White (#FFFFFF) with thick black outline (stroke) for high contrast
- Accent: Electric Green (#39FF14) or Hot Pink (#FF69B4) for playful touches
- Gold (#FFD700) for premium/special elements

**Background Colors (if using solid backgrounds):**
- Vibrant Red: #E31C1C
- Electric Green: #0BDA51
- Deep Purple: #6A0DAD (for modern party vibe)

### Spacing & Layout
- Bolder, bigger text than Version A (more impactful)
- Can cover up to 50% of image with text (more assertive)
- Use outlines/strokes generously (ensure readability)
- Dynamic layouts OK (tilted text, scattered elements)

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

**This carousel will be successful if:**
- CTR (Click-Through Rate) > 1.5% (higher than Version A)
- CPC (Cost Per Click) < $0.45 (lower than Version A)
- Swipe-through rate > 50% (users view 3+ cards)
- Engagement (likes, shares, comments) > 3% of impressions
- Positive comments mentioning "can't wait", "looks fun", "I'm there"
- Higher volume of RSVPs than Version A

**Hypothesis:**
Party-forward carousel leads with excitement and fun, attracting social/entertainment-motivated audiences. Higher swipe-through rate due to curiosity and FOMO. May generate more total RSVPs but slightly lower charity engagement (fewer toys per attendee).

## OPTIMIZATION NOTES

**After 2-3 Days of Running:**

**If Card 1 has low click-through:**
- Test different hook (food vs party vs date)
- Add countdown element ("5 Days Away!")

**If Swipe-through rate is low (<40%):**
- Make Card 1 more intriguing/mysterious
- Add explicit "Swipe to see the menu" CTA

**If Card 2 (food) has high drop-off:**
- Food might not be appealing enough - reshoot
- OR food appeal working but next cards less interesting - strengthen Cards 3-4

**If Overall CTR is low:**
- Primary text might be too long - test shorter variant
- CTA button might be wrong - test "RSVP" vs "Learn More"
