## EDITING TIPS

**Pacing:**
- Quick cuts (2-5 seconds per segment in beginning)
- Slow down for track record and CTA (more time to read/absorb)
- Dynamic but not frantic

**Color Grading:**
- Warm, inviting tones
- Consistent look across all clips
- Slightly increase saturation for visual pop
- Green/gold color accents to match brand

**Transitions:**
- Clean cuts (minimal fancy transitions)
- Simple fades or wipes between major sections
- Match action cuts where possible

**Audio:**
- Normalize all audio levels (Seren's voice should be consistent volume)
- Remove background noise/hum if present
- Add subtle room tone or background ambience if clips feel too dead

**Captions:**
- Use auto-captioning tool (CapCut, Descript, or Premiere Pro auto-transcribe)
- Proofread for accuracy (fix "Cana" ‚Üí "Kanna", etc.)
- 2-3 words per caption card max (easy to read quickly)
