### Key Discovery:
**Sojourner Center serves teens up to 17 years old** - not just little kids. Most people don't know this.

### The Problem with Original Concept:
1. **Anti-shopping messaging** - Positioned against consumerism during biggest shopping day

3. **Wrong audience** - Alienated shoppers instead of empowering them
4. **Little kid focus** - Didn't highlight teen needs (13-17 years old)
5. **Preachy tone** - "Skip shopping" is guilt-trippy and unrealistic

### Why New Concept Works Better:
1. **Educational** - Teaches what most people don't know (teens need donations too)
2. **Empowering** - "Use those deals for good" vs "Skip shopping"
3. **Practical** - Specific shopping list of teen-appropriate items
4. **Timely** - Meets people where they are (shopping on Black Friday)
5. **Actionable** - Clear next steps with one week to shop and donate
6. **Positive** - Celebrates smart shopping when it's for a cause
