## DESIGN SYSTEM (APPLIES TO ALL 5 SLIDES)

### Dimensions:
- **Size:** 1080 x 1080 pixels (Instagram square format)
- **Resolution:** 72 DPI (web) / 300 DPI (if printing)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** Forest green (#2D5016), cannabis green (#4CAF50)
- **Accent:** Holiday red (#C41E3A), festive gold (#FFD700)
- **Background:** Cream (#FFF8DC), deep red, or gradient
- **Text:** White or black (high contrast for readability)

### Typography:
- **Headers:** Bold, festive fonts (Impact, Bebas Neue, or custom holiday fonts)
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Helvetica, Arial, Open Sans)
- **Emphasis:** Hand-drawn or brush fonts for "KannaKickback"

### Visual Elements (Consistent Across Slides):
- KannaKlaus character (mascot)
- Cannabis leaf motifs (as snowflakes or decorations)
- Toy/gift box imagery
- Festive holiday elements (ornaments, lights, etc.)
- Brand hashtag: #KK6

### Branding Rules:
- **All K's, no C's:** KannaKrew, KannaKickback, KannaKlaus
- Consistent color scheme across all 5 slides
- Include small KannaKrew logo on each slide (bottom corner)
- Maintain festive but professional vibe

### Option 1: AI Generation Tools

**Recommended Tools:**
1. **ChatGPT (DALL-E)** - Best for quick iterations
2. **Midjourney** - Best for high-quality illustrations
3. **Canva** - Best for templates + text overlay
4. **Adobe Firefly** - Best for Adobe users

**Process:**
1. Use prompts above for each slide
2. Generate 2-3 versions of each
3. Select best version
4. Add text overlays in Canva or Photoshop
5. Export as 1080x1080 JPG
6. Review all 5 slides together for consistency
7. Final approval from team
8. Upload to Instagram as carousel
