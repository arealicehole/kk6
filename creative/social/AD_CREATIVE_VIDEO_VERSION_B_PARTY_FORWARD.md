## SUCCESS CRITERIA

**This ad will be successful if:**
- CTR (Click-Through Rate) > 2.0% (higher than Version A)
- CPC (Cost Per Click) < $0.40 (lower than Version A)
- Viewer retention > 60% at 10-second mark
- Completion rate > 35%
- Higher volume of RSVPs than Version A
- Positive comments mentioning "fun", "excited", "can't wait"

**Hypothesis:**
Party-forward messaging will attract viewers looking for social/entertainment experiences, resulting in higher overall RSVP volume. May have lower cost per click and broader appeal, though RSVPs might be slightly lower quality (some people interested in party who forget about toy donation).

## TONE & BRAND VOICE NOTES

**Version B should feel:**
- Energetic but not chaotic
- Fun but not immature
- Inclusive and welcoming
- Slightly cheeky/playful (KannaKlaus winking, "bring the vibes")
